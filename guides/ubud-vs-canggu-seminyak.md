# Ubud vs Canggu vs Seminyak: Where's the Actual Property Yield in Bali (2026)?

Everyone has an opinion on where to buy in Bali. Here's a framing, with bias disclosed.

- **Seminyak:** mature, expensive land, high ADR but high competition and highest entry — yields compressed.
- **Canggu:** the hype zone. Strong ADR and demand, but land prices have run hard and supply is flooding — great for appreciation bets, tougher on cap rate today.
- **Ubud:** cheaper land than the coast, year-round wellness/long-stay demand rather than one beach season. Lower ADR, but lower entry — and that ratio drives cap rate.

The bias: Ubud quietly does the better cap-rate math because you pay much less for land while well-managed occupancy holds up.
