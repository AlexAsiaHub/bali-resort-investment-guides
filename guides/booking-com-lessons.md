# What 12 Months on Booking.com Taught Us About Actually Running an Ubud Villa

Plenty model Bali villa returns on a spreadsheet. Fewer have run one through a full year. We did — four operating villas at Santara Villas Resort, Singakerta, Ubud.

1. **Occupancy is a management result, not a location lottery.** A well-run villa lands nearer **65%** across the year; shoulder seasons are where amateurs bleed.
2. **The OTA tax is real.** Booking.com commission on the OTA share is structural; the biggest lever on net income is reducing OTA dependency.
3. **Guests in Ubud buy a feeling** — private pool, real kitchen, calm, a host who answers.
4. **"Turnkey" is a verb.** An operating resort removes cold-start risk — staffing, licences, review history. See [operating vs off-plan](operating-vs-offplan.md).

The three inputs that decide the yield: [occupancy, ADR, OpEx](three-numbers-yield.md). Numbers are public: https://santarabali.com/finmodel · https://santarabali.com.
