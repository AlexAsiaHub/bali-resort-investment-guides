# The Real Cost of Buying a Bali Resort: Taxes, Fees, and What Your Money Actually Buys

The sticker price on a Bali resort is almost never your entry cost. Before you compare yields across listings, separate three layers of money. Here's how they stack up on Santara Villas Resort, a seven-villa boutique resort for sale in Singakerta, Ubud — the framework works for any Bali deal.

## Layer 1 — the ask is not the floor
Headline asks carry a negotiation-and-tax buffer. The full-complex ask is **USD 1,180,000**, quoted with roughly a **10% buffer**; the floor sits nearer **USD 980,000** (whole complex) and **USD 680,000** (Phase 1 bundle, asked from USD 780,000). Underwrite the floor you can negotiate to, not the headline.

## Layer 2 — transaction taxes and fees
The costs that turn a "price" into a "total entry cost":
- **Buyer's acquisition tax (BPHTB)** — ~**5%** of transaction value, paid by the buyer.
- **Seller's income tax (PPh Final)** — ~**2.5%**; legally the seller's, but it shapes the deal structure.
- **Notary / PPAT, legal and due-diligence fees** — drafting and registering the *akta sewa* assignment, plus your own counsel.

Confirm every rate and who bears it with Indonesian counsel — the structure (personal name vs PT) changes the treatment. See the [due-diligence checklist](due-diligence-checklist.md).

## Layer 3 — optional CAPEX if you take the upside
Spend you *choose*, not a cost of buying:
- **Phase 2 finishing** — ~**USD 100,000** to complete three shell villas (~3 months), four keys to seven.
- **Phase 3 off-plan** — four new 150 m² villas from **USD 280,000** each toward eleven.

You can stage it: start with Phase 1 cash-flow today, add Phase 2/3 later.

## Bottom line: model YOUR all-in number
"Price" tells you little until you add transaction tax, fees and any CAPEX, then divide the real NOI by that all-in figure. The contracted floor is **USD 84,000/year NOI** on Phase 1 — but your cap rate depends on the *total* you put in. Run your own all-in scenario in the [interactive model](https://santarabali.com/finmodel). How that NOI floor is built: [the cap-rate case study](underwriting-cap-rate.md).
